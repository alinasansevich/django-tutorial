Image formats

  1. gif: small, can be animated, but it can also become pixelated
    • Does well compressing large areas of a single color
    • Limited color space of 256 colors
    • Can do transparency, with jagged edges
    • Can have multiple frames, and make a little movie
  2. svg: the best, use it if you can
    • it's a vector = it can be as large as you want, won't lose quality
  3. jpg: great for compressing photos
    • Can be compressed, DO NOT put a XXX megapixel jpg on your website
    • can make the website VERY slow: needs to be prepared for the web
  4. png: when you need traansparency, good at compression, used for photos and images
    • Images that need transparency
    • Good at compression
    • Photos and images
    • Depends on the use case
